Biography

Dr. Ye Zheng is currently a algorithm engineer at JD.com. His research interests include computer vision and artificial intelligence. Specially, his research mainly focuses on 2D/3D object detection, zero-shot learning, unsupervised anomaly detection, etc.

Before that, he obtained his Ph.D degree in Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, in June 2022, supervised by Prof. Li Cui.
